Use a Strong, Unique Password

Pick one account today — your email or bank — and change its password to something 12+ characters long with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Use a password manager so you never have to remember it.

💡 Try: use 3 random words + numbers e.g. "Mango#River9Cloud"
